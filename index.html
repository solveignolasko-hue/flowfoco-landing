How it works

A study session in 3 steps

1

Pick a session

Browse available rooms by time, duration, and language. Sessions run 24/7 — morning, night, weekends. There's always a spot.

2

Set your goal & show up

Tell the room what you're working on. Turn your camera on. See the others doing the same. The timer starts — everyone gets to work.

3

Share what you got done

At the end, the room does a quick 2-minute share. You celebrate each other's wins, build your streak, and book the next session.

Why FlowFoco
